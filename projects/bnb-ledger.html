Who are

airbnb power hosts?

Airbnb power hosts are people that are serious about their Airbnb business. They often manage multiple listings and invest time and money into growing their revenue on the platform. They are often frustrated by the lack of analytics provided by Airbnb.

Metrics that are important to them include daily and monthly revenue rates, occupancy rates and profit and loss. They'd like to have a place where they are able to see how their bottom line is affected with each new booking.

solutions

Airbnb power hosts are often managing multiple properties at once and need a place where they can see their total revenue.

They also need to be able to isolate each property to understand their proportion of the total value.

Occupancy trends are also important.

Drilling in to a property allows the host to focus and isolate problems.
