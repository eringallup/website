project 1: site redesign

This was Creative Live's site when I arrived at the company. They had just redesigned their logo with an outside agency and were looking to update the site to make their users feel more at home.

the app : home

The app is designed for the job seeker needing a low friction, discrete way of showing interest in new opportunities. Upon sign up the user enters the url of their Linkedin profile, which allows the app to scan keywords from their profile to pre-qualify them for posted jobs.

preferences

The seeker sets their preferences to filter their mandatory requirements like team size, commute time, company type and perks.

posted job

The user can then browse positions that are a match for them. A short, tidy overview area keeps descriptions succinct.

swiping up

Swiping up gives more details of the team and office location.

Instead of responsibilites, projects are listed to give the seeker an exact idea of what they would be doing.

company info & perks

Financial information and leadership links allow the user to browse deeper if they'd like to know more about the company.

Perks include the must-haves already selected by the seeker in preferences.

Who are

creative learners?

Creative learners are the arty people among us. They are free spirits managing their 9-5 jobs but are dreaming of a life where they do what they love and get paid for it. They are ready to make the leap but aren't sure exactly how to make that happen.

They are people who love to learn new things. They are working photographers, musicians, crafters and graphic designers looking to hone thier crafts and grow their business skills.

Creative learners are visual, optimistic people who get bogged down by too many choices and lots of reading. They learn best by being told and shown. They like to learn alongside others and feel a part of a community.
