before

The ‘watch now’ area was a pull down tab at the top of the page. It had a dark feeling to it. The rest of the navigation was cluttered with text.

In addition,the lime green color used around the site clashed with the course graphics.

before

The calendar used was drawn in from Google. It was overwhelming to read as many classes happened at the same time and had long titles that were cut off.

final design

The final design which tested best with users implemented the ‘watch now’ list area at the top of every page and inserted static information below the navigation.

‘watch now’ pull down

calendar v.1

My initial design for the new calendar used a ‘tv guide’ model. My hunch was that users wanted to see what was on now and later on in the week.

However after its release, the calendar had negative feedback from users. We had to research what users were using the calendar to do.

calendar test, #1

This design was used to see if the users wanted a small calendar to help them put time into perspective...

calendar test, #2

... or if they wanted a month-style view of the calendar.

calendar test, #3

The third design option was most popular and showed us what users were using the calendar for: to show them a list of what was upcoming and allow them to easily get reminders for it.

Who are

creative learners?

Creative learners are the arty people among us. They are free spirits managing their 9-5 jobs but are dreaming of a life where they do what they love and get paid for it. They are ready to make the leap but aren't sure exactly how to make that happen.

They are people who love to learn new things. They are working photographers, musicians, crafters and graphic designers looking to hone thier crafts and grow their business skills.

Creative learners are visual, optimistic people who get bogged down by too many choices and lots of reading. They learn best by being told and shown. They like to learn alongside others and feel part of a community.

Who is

creative live?

Creative Live is a startup that creates long form educational video classes for people in creative fields. Classes touch all aspects of a creative's career- from improving techniques and mastering software to improving sales and marketing. Users can watch classes free when they are broadcast or purchase any time access.

I worked at Creative Live for over a year, redesigning their website and working on features like the calendar, mobile app and video classroom.
